Intro

This is an informal education platform for learning Kurdish language (Sorani dialect) through English and Finnish. It is firstly designed for children's learning. Apart from self-made content, it includes a collection of materials, links and videos deemed useful.

Here you can learn the Sorani dialect (Kurdî Nawend/Central Kurdish), which serves as the official dialect of Kurdistan in Iraq. This dialect is predominantly used in Erbil, the capital, and nearby regions.
