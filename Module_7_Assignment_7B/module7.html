About SwS


Sustainable Welfare System (SwS) is an enterprise resource planning system or software that supports social welfare organizations to manage their operations, streamline workflow, improve workplace wellbeing, and bring value to end users. The ERP system enables organizations to efficiently run their operations, for example, case work management, organization’s financials, marketing, and other everyday activities of the organizations.


The product SwS is for any social welfare organization, whether these include foster care group homes, child protection services, senior care, special needs, family work, integration services, services for homeless, and more. The ERP system is customizable to the needs of the public, private, and third sector organizations. SwS team ensures training in the use of software to maximize its benefits to the individual needs of organizations.


Our Values


MISSION: The mission of SwS is to empower social welfare organizations with an enterprise resource planning software and system that enhances workplace wellbeing, boosts job satisfaction, and improves service delivery to create greater value for their communities.


VISION: The vision of the company is to foster workplace wellbeing as a cornerstone of sustainable development, aiming to achieve measurable progress by 2030 in alignment with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). By that year, the company strives to improve the health and wellbeing of employees in social welfare organizations (SDG 3), enhance job satisfaction and retention (SDG 8), promote innovation and digital transformation in social welfare systems (SDG 9), and ensure organizations are better equipped to meet the needs of their communities (SDG 11). Additionally, SwS seeks to strengthen justice, transparency, and accountability in social welfare operations (SDG 16) while fostering partnerships to achieve these goals (SDG 17). Through its solutions, the company aims to create thriving work environments that benefit employees and contribute to a more equitable, sustainable, and impactful world.
